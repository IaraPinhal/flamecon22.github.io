SPEAKERS

Vita Ayala

Vita Ayala is a writer out of New York City, who has done work for DC Comics (Rebirth Wonder Woman Annual #1, Suicide Squad Most Wanted: Amanda Waller & El Diablo) and Image Comics (Bitch Planet Triple Feature #4), as well as penning creator owned work for Black Mask Studios (Our Work Fills the Pews). When not writing, they moonlight protecting art at one of the city's oldest and largest museums.

Kate Leth

Canadian writer and artist Kate Leth specializes in teen and all ages stories. Her superhero/romance genre-blending Marvel series Patsy Walker, A.K.A. Hellcat! received a 2017 GLAAD Media Award nomination. Kate’s previous work includes three Adventure Time original graphic novels including New York Times Best Seller Adventure Time: Seeing Red, Ink for Beginners: A Comic Guide to Getting Tattooed, Fresh Romance, Girl Over Parisand Power Up.

Crystal Frasier

Crystal Frasier is a writer, game developer, and graphic designer with eighteen years’ experience, best known for her work on the Pathfinder line of roleplaying games and adventures. She strives to make games worlds where everyone can see themselves reflected. She is a survivor of both the Art Institute of Seattle and New College of Florida, and pulls heavy inspiration for her work from European, Central American, and African history as well as the works of L. Frank Baum and Lewis Caroll. In her free time, Crystal reads comics, plays with her dog, and obsessively rewatches old cartoons from the 80s and 90s.

Wendy Xu

Wendy Xu is a Brooklyn-based illustrator and comics artist with three upcoming graphic novels from Harper Collins.She is the co-creator of "Mooncakes", a young adult fantasy graphic novel published in 2019 from Lion Forge Comics. Her work has been featured on Catapult, Barnes & Noble Sci-fi/Fantasy Blog, and Tor.com, among other places. You can find more art on her instagram: @artofwendyxu or on twitter: @angrygirLcomics

Magdalene Visaggio

Mags Visaggio writes queer-inclusive sci fi comics including the critically acclaimed Quantum Teens Are Go! and 2017 Eisner Award nominee Kim and Kim, published by Black Mask Studios. The sequel series Kim and Kim: Love is a Battlefield began publication in July. Mags just made her Marvel debut with a Starbrand story in Secret Empire: Brave New World #3, and her take on a Venomized Rocket Racoon will appear in Venomverse: War Stories in September.

Steve Orlando

Steve Orlando writes comics, including the GLAAD-Award-Nominated Midnighter and Midnighter and Apollo, Justice League of America, Batman / The Shadow, and VIRGIL.
